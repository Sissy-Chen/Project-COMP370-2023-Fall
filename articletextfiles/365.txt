The past few weeks have seen Taylor Swift making frequent trips to NFL games, particularly to watch the Kansas City Chiefs and their star player, Travis Kelce. As her attendance at football games has become a topic of discussion among the fan bases of both stars, it has led to an intriguing comparison.

Interestingly, this caught the eye of former Love Island star Georgina Townend, who made an interesting comparison. She retweeted and wrote, “What Taylor Swift is doing for the NFL is like what Ryan Reynolds did for Wrexham FC.”
