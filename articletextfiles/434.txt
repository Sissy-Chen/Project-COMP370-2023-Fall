The former White House communications director for Trump, Alyssa Farah Griffin, has made a humorous suggestion that Swift could be the ‘only person’ capable of defeating Trump in the 2024 presidential elections. During a recent podcast, Griffin expressed her admiration for Swift. She also shared her belief that Swift’s influence and appeal to a broad demographic, especially young voters, could make her a formidable candidate. She stated: “If Donald Trump looks like he’s gonna win, she’s just gonna need to get in the race and defeat him once and for all, because she’s probably the only person who can.”

Rumors surrounding the alleged romance between Swift and NFL star Kelce have reached the White House, prompting questions during a recent press briefing. White House Press Secretary Karine Jean-Pierre introduced National Security Council official John Kirby to address the media.
When asked about the rumored relationship, Kirby humorously replied that he could neither confirm nor deny the reports and offered to take the question back to their analysts. The ongoing speculation has generated significant media attention, with neither Swift nor Kelce officially confirming their relationship.
