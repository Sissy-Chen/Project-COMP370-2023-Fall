It’s undeniable that ever since Tay-Tay’s appearance at Arrowhead Stadium, fans have been eagerly anticipating her presence in New York as well. With her massive following, she brought the NFL gift of a huge viewership boost. The last time a number had come close was during Super Bowl LVII, which aired on February 12 this year, with an average viewership was approximately 115.1 million, per the reports.

Taylor Swift’s popularity is so immense that even celebrities and friends couldn’t resist joining the scene on Sunday. The “Bad Blood” singer was accompanied by superstars such as Blake Lively, Lively’s husband Ryan Reynolds, Sophie Turner, Shawn Levy, Hugh Jackman, and Paul Rudd. The Sunday night football game felt like a gathering of the Marvel stars!
Additionally, the guest list included Aaron Rodgers, who is currently recovering from an injury and appeared with crutches to support the Jets. Another highlight of the showdown was the Chiefs QB Patrick Mahomes, who achieved a historic milestone by recording the fastest 200 touchdown passes in NFL history in just 84 games. This accomplishment solidifies Mahomes’ reputation as the Young G.O.A.T of the NFL.
